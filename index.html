App Safety

Transparency about how Approach Pal handles your data — aligned with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.

Last updated: March 2026

🎯 Purpose of This Policy

This Safety Policy is provided to meet Google Play Store's Data Safety disclosure requirements. It describes the types of data Approach Pal collects, how that data is used, shared, and secured, and what controls you have as a user.

For the full details of our privacy practices, please also read our Privacy Policy.

📋 Data Collected & Why

The table below summarises every data type Approach Pal may collect:

Data Type Collected Purpose
Name Yes Personalise the coaching experience
Email address Yes Account authentication & service communications
User-generated content (chat messages) Yes Provide context-aware AI coaching responses
App activity (missions, progress) Yes Display stats and track personal growth
App interaction & crash logs Yes Diagnose bugs and improve stability (Firebase)
Purchase history / subscription status Yes Manage premium access via RevenueCat
Precise location No —
Contacts or photos No —
Health or biometric data No —

🔗 Data Sharing

We share limited data only with the following trusted third-party providers required to operate the app:

  • Firebase / Google — authentication, cloud database, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Payment details are handled by the app store (Google Play) and never passed to us.
  • OpenAI — AI responses are generated by processing your chat messages. OpenAI's API data policies apply.

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties for commercial purposes.

🛡️ Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS / TLS).
  • Data is stored in Firebase with strict security rules limiting access to authenticated users only.
  • Authentication is handled via Firebase Auth — passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by contacting us at support@approachpal.app.

Upon a verified deletion request, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you may be retained.

🔞 Age Requirements

Approach Pal is intended for users aged 13 and older. The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account and all associated data.

✅ Your Controls

As a user of Approach Pal, you can:

  • Access and review your personal data by contacting support.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request full deletion of your account and data.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription at any time via Google Play subscriptions.
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling analytics permissions in your device settings.

📝 Policy Updates

This Safety Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notification.
